Our Academic Programs

At Harvard School, we are committed to a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum that prepares students for both higher education and lifelong success. Our programs span various subjects, including:

  • Pre-primary Education: Focused on building strong foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.
  • Primary School: Emphasizing intellectual curiosity, independent learning, and a well-rounded education.
  • Junior School: Offering specialized programs that prepare students for college and professional careers, including electives and advanced courses.

Curriculum Overview

Our curriculum is designed to align with the Competence-Based Curriculum (CBC) and includes a balanced approach that combines theoretical knowledge with practical learning experiences. Subjects include:

  • Mathematics
  • Science and Technology
  • Social Studies
  • Creative Arts and Sports
  • English
  • Kiswahili
  • German
  • French
  • Mandarin
  • Religious Education
