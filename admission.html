Admissions

How to Apply

To join Harvard School, prospective students and their families can submit an online application or visit the admissions office for assistance. We strive to make the application process straightforward and welcoming.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Pre-primary: 3-5 years
  • Primary: 6-13 years
  • Junior School: 13-17 years

Admission Forms

Application forms can be downloaded directly from our website or collected from the admissions office. Complete the form and submit it with the required documents by the given deadline.
