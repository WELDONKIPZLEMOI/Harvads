About Us

Overview of Harvard School

Harvard School was founded in 2012 with the vision of providing a transformative and inclusive educational experience. Our history began when Michael Emuria, the principal, and a group of dedicated educators came together to create a school that nurtures academic excellence and character development. Since its inception, Harvard School has grown to become a beacon of quality education, inspiring generations of students.

Mission

Our mission is to provide an enriching learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and personal growth. We strive to empower our students to excel academically and become responsible and compassionate leaders in their communities.

Leadership

Harvard School is led by Principal Michael Emuria and a team of experienced educators who bring a wealth of knowledge and passion for teaching.
